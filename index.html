Our Story

Born from a Love of Fragrance & Form

Sable & Bloom was founded in Paris in 2019 by two perfume lovers who believed that the ritual of wearing fragrance deserved a vessel as beautiful as the scent itself. Every bag in our collection is handcrafted by artisans in Tuscany using only the finest materials — sumptuous leathers, hand-dyed velvet, and silk sourced from centuries-old ateliers.

We design for the woman who understands that beauty is in the details — the weight of gold hardware, the whisper of satin lining, the way a perfect bag makes your morning ritual feel like ceremony.

Each piece is made in limited quantities, ensuring that your Sable & Bloom bag is as rare and personal as your signature scent.

FAQ

Questions, Answered

What sizes of perfume bottles do your bags accommodate? +
Our bags are designed to hold standard perfume bottles ranging from 30ml to 100ml. Each product listing specifies the exact capacity. The Or Lumière Set includes a travel pouch for 10–30ml decant bottles.
What materials are used in your bags? +
We use only premium materials: full-grain and Nappa leather from Tuscan tanneries, hand-dyed silk and velvet, 24K gold-plated brass hardware, and silk or satin interior linings. All materials are ethically sourced and cruelty-free.
How do I care for my Sable & Bloom bag? +
We recommend storing your bag in the dust pouch provided, away from direct sunlight. For leather bags, use a high-quality leather conditioner every 3–6 months. For velvet styles, gently brush with a soft cloth to restore the pile. Avoid contact with water and perfume spillage.
Do you offer international shipping? +
Yes — we ship to over 18 countries worldwide. Standard international shipping takes 7–14 business days. Express options are available at checkout. All orders are fully insured and tracked.
What is your return and exchange policy? +
We offer a 14-day return policy for unworn, unused items in their original packaging. To initiate a return, please contact us at returns@sableandbloom.com. Exchanges can be arranged within 21 days of purchase. Sale items and limited-edition pieces are final sale.
Can I personalise or gift-wrap my order? +
Absolutely. We offer complimentary signature gift wrapping with every order. For personalisation — including monogramming and custom colour commissions — please email us at hello@sableandbloom.com with your request. Personalised orders typically require 2–3 additional weeks.
